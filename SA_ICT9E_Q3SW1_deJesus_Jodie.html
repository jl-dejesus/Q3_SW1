The Adventures of Hello Kitty & Friends





Synopsis:

The Adventures of Hello Kitty & Friends is a 3D CGI television show. It stars Hello Kitty and her friends, and depicts the adventures in their day-to-day lives. Like many kids shows, it teaches colors, shapes, and basic maths. It also teaches values like kindness, honesty, courage, and teamwork.


Characters:

Hello Kitty

The cute, cheerful, lively, and friendly protagonist of this series. Hello Kitty loves nothing more than apples and adventures with her friends. She learns new things everyday with her friends' help, support, and kindness.



My Melody

My Melody is Hello Kitty's best friend. She is shy, quiet, but never afraid to stand up for her friends. She loves to sew and bake delicious treats to enjoy with everyone she loves.



Dear Daniel

A classy, gentle, and considerate fellow. Dear Daniel admires Hello Kitty and he enjoys spending most of his time with her. He is always there to help Hello Kitty and his other friends with their schoolwork. Of course, he is also there whenever they need help with other problems. Dear Daniel is a reliable friend.



Badtz Maru

He may be rowdy, he may be chaotic, and he may be reckless, but Badtz Maru cares for all of his friends. He is an enthusiast of games, sports, and everything cool. He is competitive, always ready to win, and a good sport...most of the time.



Pochacco

Pochacco is a happy-go-lucky little guy. He likes to go on walks, to eat banana ice cream, or to do both at the same time. He also plays soccer. Pochacco loves sharing the things he enjoys with his friends.



Keroppi

With his bright personality, Keroppi never fails to brighten the mood. He is a great singer and can be quite cheeky at times. He has a knack for making boats out of flowers and plants.
